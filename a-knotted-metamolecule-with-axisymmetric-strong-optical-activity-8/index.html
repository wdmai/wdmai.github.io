Task-oriented reconfigurable metasurfaces based on inverse design and temporal meta-systems

Dr. Wending Mai

This chapter is organized as follows. In Section 7.2, innovative work on the optimization-enabled GST metasurfaces for sophisticated beam switching and control is highlighted. In Section 7.3, recent developments in the field of temporal artificial media are first reviewed, and then recent work on the time-varying anisotropic materials for complete polarization conversion is summarized. Finally, in Section 7.4, conclusions and a scope for future research are provided.
